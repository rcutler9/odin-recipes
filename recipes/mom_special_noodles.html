Mom's Special Noodles

go back

Description

This is momma's special noodle recipe. It has been passed down for generations to make the amazing recipe you're about to see. No substitutions are allowed in this recipe; ya make what momma cooked up, or you ain't eatin' nothin'at all.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • butter
  • salt
  • pepper
  • bouillion
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder

Instructions

  1. cook noodles in water
  2. drain water
  3. put in butter
  4. mix
  5. put in salt, pepper, bouillion, garlic powder, and onion powder
  6. mix again
  7. eat
